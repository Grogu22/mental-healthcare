WELCOME TO UNITY

A state-of-the art modern facility in the heart of Kolkata, it is spread over 10 acres and has a built-up area of over 440000 square feet. Currently the hospital has 300+ beds with a capacity to expand to 400 beds.

Unity Mental Hospital, Kolkata is a mental healthcare unit of the Unity Hospitals Group. Rehabilitation and treatment of drug addicts is also done here.

Unity Mental Hospital, Kolkata provides holistic healthcare that includes prevention, treatment, rehabilitation and health education for patients, their families and clients by touching their lives.
